DOTS AND BOXES MiNUS THE DOTS

August 4, 2024

so much is new this week that i kinda don't even know where to start. so i'm going to start at the start

  • brand new rounded corner design. they aren't even rounded corners at all, now they're slanted corners. doesn't that look so cool?!?! not everything is perfect, but i'm still quite happy with where i am.
  • added a little bit of grain to elements. i've already noiced that grain is becoming the new 45 degree material design drop shadow, so i decided to try not to overdo it
  • massive performance improvements. sadly, some effects had to go. the parallax was cool when it worked, but 90% of the time it would just stutter. the effect where the color changes on the homepage is also gone, as it was really resource intensive and didn't even look that cool anyway
  • the mobile experience is also a little better. i got one complaint saying that running my website on an older phone causes it to catch fire, so i did a lot of performance improvements for mobile. that new cool corner design isn't on mobile
  • there is some brand new stuff in the more page. just graphs and stuff, but its still cool.

overall, this is awesome, and i am really happy that i was able to get this new design to work. you have no idea how long i spent trying to make variably sized boxed corners
