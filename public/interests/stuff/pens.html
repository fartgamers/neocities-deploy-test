FAVORiTE PENS

This is a list of my favorite pens.

Pilot G2 07

In terms of color, my favorite pen is the Pilot G2 07. I like them because the color is bright and vibrant, and the pen writes really smooth. I especially like the red.

Sharpie S Gel

For casual writing in black, I like using a Sharpie S Gel pen. They write really smooth, and are fun to use.
