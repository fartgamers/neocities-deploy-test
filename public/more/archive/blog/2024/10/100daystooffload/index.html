#100DaysToOffload

October 5, 2024

I am looking at participating in #100DaysToOffload. This is a challenge where you publish 100 posts to your personal blog in a year.

Why?

I have been wanting to get more into journaling and writing. I think doing this challenge would be a fun way to encourage myself to write more, outside of just my personal diary. I also rarely ever finish projects, so I think forcing myself to publish 100 blog posts within a year would be a fun way to try and complete a project.
These 100 posts do not have to be a streak, that would be unreasonable. I cannot write a post every single day, I have a life outside this website. Sometimes I may write a post, but not publish it on this site on the same day. That is fine too, as long as I publish it eventually. Rules don't state if I can publish multiple posts for the same date, but I am going to count only one post per day for this.
Also, these articles don't have to necessarily be of top quality. It's more about getting into the habit of writing blog posts. I am reaching for at least 200 words per article, however.
