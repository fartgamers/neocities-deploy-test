• advice for the future generation

    blurbs of advice that i wish i was told
  • if someones whole thing is that they "spread a forbidden message", it's probably best to ignore them
  • the first day of work will always be one of the worst. you will constantly have a feeling of impending doom. it will get better after a couple days
  • always make backups and save extra copies. you won't know what you miss until it's gone. if you see something that you like, save it
  • speaking of that, when moving schools or when graduating, make a backup of your entire school google account. this will let you look back at your school projects, emails, and everything
  • if something will take less than 5 minutes to do and you can do it, just do it. don't get in the habit of having an overwhelming backlog of tasks to complete
